What you get

Tools built around
how you see the world.

Visibly is built for colorblind designers who want to ship work that lands the way they intended it — for everyone.

See what others see
Simulate how your design appears to non-colorblind users across all four major vision types — so you know exactly where your intent gets lost.
Clarity score
A 0–100 score shows how clearly your design communicates to the broadest possible audience, broken down by vision type.
Prioritized issue list
Issues ranked by severity with the exact colors involved. High, medium, and low priority — so you know what to fix first.
Safe color suggestions
4 alternative palettes that are colorblind-safe and visually close to your original intent. Not random — intentional swaps.
Screenshot upload
Drag and drop any PNG, JPG, or WebP. No account needed, no integrations, no setup. Just drop and scan.
Figma plugin — coming soon
Scan directly from your canvas without leaving Figma. Token-level fixes, component breakdowns, and Systemly integration.
